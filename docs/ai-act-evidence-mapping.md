# AI Act Evidence Mapping

This document is an implementation-oriented evidence map, not legal advice.

## Why Map Evidence

Teams often need to assemble governance evidence across product, engineering, and compliance workflows. AgentLock can help by keeping release artifacts structured and portable.

## Example Mapping

- `genome.yaml`
  Captures intended purpose, operating constraints, tools, and data-handling defaults.
- `behavior.contract.yaml`
  Captures release criteria, approval requirements, and quality thresholds.
- `agent.lock.yaml`
  Captures resolved dependencies, integrity digests, and rollback constraints.
- trace events
  Capture execution evidence, policy decisions, tool usage, and approval requests.
- replay manifests and reports
  Capture post-change validation inputs and comparison methodology.
- release attestation
  Captures signed release outcome and evidence references.

## Practical Use

An implementation can use these artifacts to assemble:

- release dossiers
- audit-ready evidence packets
- internal control reviews
- change-management approvals

The exact mapping depends on the deployer, jurisdiction, and system classification.
